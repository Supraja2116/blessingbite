How BlessingBite Works

Our simple three-step process to reduce waste and feed the hungry

1. Post Donation

Enter details about your surplus food - type, quantity, and pickup location.

2. Match & Assign

We match with nearby NGOs and assign a delivery partner (auto or truck).

3. Track & Earn

Monitor the delivery in real-time and earn Blessing Points for your contribution.
